# Tadarruj | تدرج

### Your Calm Path to Success — طريقك الهادئ نحو النجاح

**Tadarruj** is a bilingual web platform designed to help **Saudi high school students (Grades 10–12)** navigate their academic journey with clarity and structure.

The platform transforms scattered exams, requirements, and opportunities into a **clear system of timelines, study plans, and long-term academic roadmaps**.

🌐 Live Platform  
https://tadarruj.lovable.app

# Overview

Saudi high school students often face:

- Unclear academic timelines
- Multiple exams and requirements
- Scattered information across different platforms
- Lack of long-term planning tools

**Tadarruj solves this by providing a structured academic guidance system** that turns uncertainty into a clear, step-by-step path toward university readiness.

# Key Features

### 3-Year Academic Roadmap
Personalized academic guidance from **current grade to graduation**.

### Smart Study Planner
Automatically converts **exam deadlines into daily study plans**.

### Opportunity Radar
Discover **volunteering programs, competitions, scholarships, and initiatives**.

### Important Dates Dashboard
Track **exams, application deadlines, and national events**.

### Goal Grade Tracking
Set **target grades** and monitor progress toward academic goals.

# Platform Experience

- 🌍 Web-based application
- 🌐 Full **Arabic & English support**
- 🔁 **RTL / LTR layout support**
- 👤 **Guest mode** for exploration
- 🔐 **User accounts** for roadmap generation, planning, and progress tracking

# Mission

Tadarruj helps students replace uncertainty with:

- **Clear plans**
- **Structured progress**
- **Calm academic growth**

> Success doesn’t have to be chaotic — it can be **gradual, structured, and intentional**.
